Proposal Summary

This section reframes Xybervu as a safety proposal: a preventive sensing platform that can support safer urban cycling, pilot deployment, and higher-confidence eBike and eCargo operations.

Preventive Safety Infrastructure
預防式安全基礎設施

Frame the solution as part of safer urban mobility infrastructure, not just as a rider gadget.

把方案定位成更安全城市移動的一部分，而不是單純的騎士配件。

Radar + Vision Public Value
雷達與視覺的公共價值

Use radar certainty and visual confirmation to communicate reliability, transparency, and usability in real traffic conditions.

以雷達的確定性與影像確認能力，說明它在真實交通情境中的可靠性、透明度與可用性。

Pilot-Ready Integration

Present Xybervu as a platform that can support pilots, municipal deployments, and OEM collaboration.

把 Xybervu 表述成可支援 pilot、示範導入與 OEM 協作的安全平台。

Public Use Cases

讓 eBike 與 eCargo 對政府機關更有可讀性。

Public audiences respond better to use cases that connect technology with operational safety, pilot feasibility, and urban-mobility outcomes.

Urban eBike Safety
都會 eBike 安全方案

Supports safer riding in complex traffic environments through rear-approaching vehicle awareness and more intuitive warning flow.

透過後方來車感知與更直覺的警示流程，支援都會複雜交通環境中的 eBike 安全騎乘。

eCargo Fleet Visibility
eCargo 車隊後方可視性

A stronger fit for larger vehicles that need improved rear awareness, reliable warnings, and safer interactions with surrounding traffic.

特別適合體積更大的 eCargo 載具，強化後方可視性、警示可靠性，以及與周圍車流的安全互動。

Government Pilot Programs
政府示範導入計畫

Useful for demonstration zones, urban safety initiatives, and mobility pilot programs that need a credible preventive safety story.

適合示範場域、城市安全計畫與 mobility pilot program，讓政府單位能更清楚理解預防式安全的導入價值。

Commuter eBike Safety
通勤 eBike 安全方案

Early rear-vehicle detection in busy commuter corridors reduces blind-spot incidents and builds rider confidence during peak-hour traffic.

在繁忙通勤路段提供早期後方車輛偵測，降低盲點事故風險並提升尖峰時段的騎乘信心。

Night & Low-Light eBike Safety
夜間與低光源 eBike 安全

Radar maintains reliable detection even in complete darkness while vision provides context, making night commuting significantly safer.

雷達在完全黑暗環境中仍能可靠偵測，視覺技術則提供情境理解，讓夜間通勤大幅提升安全性。

Shared Mobility eBike Safety
共享 eBike 車隊安全

Designed for municipal bike-sharing fleets and campus operators — improves operational safety, reduces liability, and increases user adoption.

專為市政共享單車車隊與校園營運設計，提升操作安全性、降低責任風險，並提高使用者採用率。
